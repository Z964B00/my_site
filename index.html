Bio

I am a customer educator and amateur coder based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. My two biggest passions are teach and learn. At one point, I believed my professional future would be in academia. I saw myself becoming a unversity professor, delivering lectures and leading tutorials so I could inspire a love for learning in my students similar to my own. After a years-long interlude working at the Toronto Humane Society following graduation (I am also a huge animal lover), I transitioned to a career in software. After taking roles in training and documentation, I found my calling Customer Education. Working in this field feeds my passion to educate while allowing my explore new learning modalities (outside of traditional academia) that integrate into flow of work and life.

Skills

Queen's University, BA (History)

From 2011 to 2013, I completed the first two years of my undergraduate degree in History at Queen's University.

University of Toronto, BA & MA (History & Philsophy of Science)

From 2013 to 2015, I completed the remaining two years of my undergraduate degree at the University of Toronto. I switched my specialization to the History and Philosophy of Science. In 2016, I completed a one year course-based Master's degree at the Institute for the History and Philosophy of Science and Technology at Victoria College.

York University, Learning Experience Design Certificate

In 2022, I completed a 6-month certificate program in Learning Experience Design at York University.

Top Hat (2018-2021)

Top Hat is an educational software company founded in 2009. The company's original product was an in-class polling tool for colleges and universities. They have since expanded their product to include digital textbooks and other forms of courseware.

Instructional Designer (Customer Onboarding)

In this role, I help post-secondary educations implement the Top Hat produt for their classes in according with instructional design best practices.

Technical Writer/Revenue Enablement Specialist

In this role, I was responsible for maintaining all of Top Hat's support documentation. I was also responsible for facilitating ongoing product training for Top Hat employees.

Lever (2021-2023)

Lever is a recruitment software company founded in 2012. The company's product is an applicant tracking system (ATS) that recruiters can use to create job postings, review resumes, schedule interviews, manage hiring decisions, and send offer letters. In 2022, Lever as acquired by Employ to be part of its talent aquisition suite of products and services, alongside Jobvite, JazzHR, and Nextthing RPO.

Content and Education Specialist

In this role, I was responsible for managing support documentation in Lever's Help Center. I also managed the delivery of instructional webinars and built the initial Administrator Certification in Lever University.

Content and Education, Team Lead

In this role, I was responsible for coordinating the efforts of the Customer Education team, including hiring the first new member of the team and working cross-functionally with other teams within Lever to review and publicize content.

Content and Education, Manager

In this role, I was responsible for overseeing the development of content in all of Lever's Customer Education channels including the Help Center, instructor-led trainings, and the customer academy.

MaintainX (2023 - present)

MaintainX is a software company founded in 2018. The company's product is a computerized maintenance management system (CMMS), designed to help manufacturing facilities manager their maintenance operations.

Manager of Customer Education

In this role, I am responsible for overseeing the development educational content and services across all of MaintainX's customer education channels including the customer academy, instructor-led training, and digital adoption tools.

Camtasia

I have multiple years of experience using Camtasia to create instructional videos. I am also completed TechSmith's Explorer and Voyager Camtasia certifications.

Learning Management Systems

I have experience working with institutional education LMSes from my time at Top Hat, including Canvas, Blackboard, and D2L. I am also well-versed at administrating customer education learning management systems, having completed implementations of both Skilljar and WorkRamp.

Code

I have been learning to code since 2022. At this time, I am proficient in HTML, CSS, Python, and basic JavaScript. I look forward to continuing my coding journey and adding more coding languages to my skill set.
